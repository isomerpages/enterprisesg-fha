			<h4 style="text-transform: uppercase; color: black;"><b>Leong Guan Food Manufacturer Pte Ltd</b></h4>
			<p>Leong Guan Food Manufacturer is Singapore's leading fresh noodle and beancurd maker. Using food science, it offers preservative-free, long shelf-life products, from local noodles and soy beancurd to Japanese ramen, Italian pasta, and Hong Kong wanton mee. Its range includes high-fiber, low-GI, vegetarian, gluten-free, ready-to-eat meals, and food kits. Proudly made in Singapore, LG Foods serves local F&amp;B, supermarkets, and exports to Asia, the Middle East, Australia, Europe, and the US.</p>
